Hotel Maestral

9.0

In romantic rocky bay of the Montenegrin Riviera, above the charming send beach, lays Maestral Resort & Casino. The name has been taken from the fresh aromatic wind that has been companion of that picturesque rocky coast through the summer months.

From its south side the “Maestral” strokes the old fisherman’s village Pržno, and the neighbor bays where are located the most beautiful royal beaches of Miločer. Vivid island of St. Stefan located more on the south, that climbs up from the crystal clean sea will at least bring you a desire to explore it.
